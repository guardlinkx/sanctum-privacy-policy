Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock premium features?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Upgrade to Premium". You can choose between monthly, yearly, or lifetime plans. New users get 3 days of free full access.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Google account used for the original purchase.

Why does Sanctum need accessibility service access?

The accessibility service is used only for the App Lock feature to detect when a locked app is opened. It does not read your screen content or keystrokes.

Why does Sanctum need SMS permissions?

SMS permissions are used exclusively for the Anti-Theft feature. If your device is lost or stolen, you can send SMS commands from a trusted phone number to locate, lock, or ring your device.

Is my data sent to any servers?

No. All data collected by Sanctum is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit, sell, or share your data with anyone.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Google Play. Open Google Play Store, tap your profile icon, go to Payments & Subscriptions, then Subscriptions, and cancel Sanctum from there.
