Sanctum Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Sanctum is a mobile security application developed by GuardLinkx Technologies LLC ("we", "our", or "the Company"). The app is designed to protect your Android device from threats, unauthorized access, and privacy violations.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using Sanctum, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. All data collected by Sanctum is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers unless explicitly stated.

2. Information We Collect

Sanctum collects the following types of information to provide its security features:

LOCAL DATA (stored only on your device):

a) App Information

  • List of installed applications
  • App permissions and security analysis
  • Installation sources and timestamps

b) Device Information

  • Device model and Android version
  • Security settings status (debug mode, unknown sources)
  • Device administrator status

c) Location Data (Anti-Theft feature)

  • GPS coordinates when Anti-Theft is enabled
  • Location is only accessed when you enable this feature
  • Used for device recovery if lost or stolen

d) Network Information

  • WiFi network names and security status
  • Network connection logs
  • VPN detection status

e) Security Events

  • Surveillance detection results
  • Threat scan history
  • App lock access attempts

f) Communication Access (with your permission)

  • SMS command processing for Anti-Theft features
  • Call forwarding detection for security monitoring
  • Phone state for SIM card change detection

3. How We Use Your Information

All collected information is used solely to provide security features:

  • App Scanning: To identify potentially harmful applications
  • Privacy Protection: To monitor and alert you about sensitive permission usage
  • Anti-Theft Protection: To help locate and secure your device if lost
  • Surveillance Detection: To identify potential spyware or monitoring software
  • Network Security: To warn you about insecure network connections
  • App Lock: To protect your sensitive applications from unauthorized access
WE DO NOT:
  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Transmit your data to external servers
  • Use your data for purposes other than security

4. Data Storage and Security

LOCAL STORAGE ONLY:

All your data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage mechanisms:

  • Encrypted SharedPreferences for sensitive settings
  • Room Database for scan history and logs
  • Data is protected by your device's security (PIN, pattern, biometrics)

DATA RETENTION:

  • Scan history: Stored until you clear app data
  • Security logs: Last 30 days
  • Location data: Only while Anti-Theft is active

You can delete all stored data at any time through: Settings → Data & Storage → Clear All Data

5. Permissions We Request

LOCATION (Fine & Background):

  • Purpose: Anti-Theft device tracking
  • When used: Only when Anti-Theft is enabled
  • You can disable this in Android Settings

ACCESSIBILITY SERVICE:

  • Purpose: App Lock functionality
  • What it does: Detects when locked apps are opened
  • Does NOT read your screen content or keystrokes

DEVICE ADMINISTRATOR:

  • Purpose: Anti-Theft remote lock/wipe
  • What it does: Allows remote device protection commands
  • You can revoke this at any time in Settings

SMS (Send & Receive):

  • Purpose: Anti-Theft SMS commands
  • What it does: Responds to trusted phone numbers for device recovery
  • Only processes commands from numbers you authorize

READ PHONE STATE:

  • Purpose: SIM card change detection for Anti-Theft protection
  • What it does: Detects when SIM card is removed or replaced
  • Helps identify potential device theft

USAGE STATS:

  • Purpose: Privacy monitoring and app lock
  • What it does: Detects which app is currently active

QUERY ALL PACKAGES:

  • Purpose: Security scanning
  • What it does: Lists installed apps for threat analysis

6. SMS Feature Disclosure

Sanctum's Anti-Theft feature includes SMS-based remote commands:

HOW IT WORKS:

  1. You configure trusted phone numbers in Anti-Theft settings
  2. If your device is lost, you can send SMS commands from a trusted number
  3. Commands include: LOCATE, LOCK, ALARM

Important:

  • SMS commands only work from phone numbers YOU authorize
  • You must explicitly enable this feature
  • Standard SMS rates from your carrier may apply
  • You can disable SMS commands at any time

We do NOT:

  • Read your personal text messages
  • Access your SMS conversations
  • Send SMS without your explicit action or command

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • ACCESS: View all collected data in the app
  • DELETE: Clear all data through Settings → Data & Storage
  • DISABLE: Turn off any feature that collects data
  • REVOKE: Remove permissions through Android Settings
  • UNINSTALL: Removing the app deletes all local data

OPTING OUT:

  • Disable Anti-Theft to stop location tracking
  • Disable App Lock to stop accessibility service
  • Revoke Device Admin to disable remote commands
  • Each feature can be individually controlled

8. Children's Privacy

Sanctum is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have the data removed.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Showing a notification in the app for significant changes

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
